Instructions

You are now starting Section 6 of the experiment. We kindly ask you to read the instructions concerning this section.

Groups and rounds: You will make decisions for a total of {{ rounds }} days. Each day, you will be matched in groups of six participants, including yourself. The group composition will change every day, meaning that you will be matched with potentially five new participants. You will not be told who the other five group members are.

How to make decisions: In each period you will be provided with an endowment of 30 Tokens. All other members of your group will also have an endowment of 30 Tokens. In each period you will have to decide how to use your Tokens. You will make your decisions individually. All group members make their own decisions at the same time.

Common account: You can allocate in the Common account any whole number X of Tokens between 0 and 30. The Tokens allocated to the Common account by your group will be first increased by a certain factor and then distributed back among all 6 group members in proportion to the amount each individual allocates. The return on your investment decreases with the total amount of Tokens your group allocates to the Common account. This means that your earnings from the Common account depend not only on the amount of Tokens you allocate but on how much each of you as a group allocate to it in a given round. You will find the earnings formula for the Common account below.

Personal account: The tokens remaining after deciding how many Tokens to allocate to the Common account are yours to keep.

Total earnings in each period: Your total earnings per period are the sum of the tokens you kept and your earnings from the Common account.

To facilitate your decision task, you will have access to an interactive tool when you have to make your decisions and a comprehensive table indicating your earnings, for each possible amount allocated by you in Common account (X) and by the all five members in your group (Y). To check the table now, click the link below

Period by period information: After each period, you will get information on the individual decisions of the other members of your group. In each period, after all participants have made their decisions you will get information on:

  1. your own allocation decision in the current round
  2. the average amount of Tokens allocated to the Common account by the other members of your group in the current round
  3. your earnings from the Common account in the current round
  4. the amount you allocate to the Private account in the current round
  5. the sum of your earnings in Common and Private account for that round

Final Payment: At the end of the experiment the system will randomly select five rounds, the first one from the first XXX rounds, the second one from the second XXX rounds, ..., the fifth one from the last XXX rounds. The amount you have gained during those five decisions will be added to your final payment. Each round is independent from each other in terms of your earnings.

Additional Questions and Points: During each round there will be some additional questions that will allow you to gain extra points. [Piece to possibly add: These questions will be displayed before you make your allocation decision in the game and will be labeled as “XXX Additional Questions” throughout the game]

At the end of the experiment, {{ num_subjects_win }} people who successfully complete it will be randomly selected to win a bonus. This bonus multiplies the earnings of those people in one randomly selected decision of Stage 6 by {{ win_multiplier }}. This means that the randomly selected people can earn up to €100 for one of their Stage 6 decisions in addition to the rest of their payoffs.

Remember, if you miss {{ inactive_threshold }} decisions during the experiment you will be permanently and automatically excluded. If a person does not make their decision in a round then a randomly chosen decision of another person in that group will be automatically implemented.
